Saratoga Gavel Club

Toastmaster Club for Youths

Give Youth the Best Gift Ever of Toastmasters

What the kids will learn

  • Overcome nervousness when speaking before an audience.
  • Organize and present ideas logically and convincing.
  • Listen carefully to others’ ideas.
  • Participate in and lead group discussion or meetings.
  • Offer advice to help others improve their speaking and
    • leadership skills.

    How it is organized

  • Who: Anyone between age 11—17 (born in 2003—1997)
  • Where: 10:30am—12:00 meet 1st, 3rd weeks of each month
  • Cost : $5/ month (to help with cost of running the club)
  • Place: Prince of Peace Lutheran Church
  • Address: 12770 Saratoga Ave, Saratoga, CA 95070
